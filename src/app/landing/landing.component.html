Rules & Regulations

  1. Wear your mask.
  2. Stay home when you are not feeling well.
  3. Wash your hand regularly.
  4. 2 metre social distancing must be maintained.
  5. Never share your mask with someone.
  6. Use hand sanitizer often.
  7. Make sure that you sanitize regularly touched object and surface.
  8. Sneeze or Cough in your belt elbow not your hands.
  9. No hugs and shaking of hands
